## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Contact Page
The Contact page SHALL provide a means for visitors to contact the developer, either through a contact form or mailto link.

#### Scenario: Contact Form Display
- **WHEN** a contact form is implemented
- **THEN** the form includes at minimum: name/email field and message field
- **AND** form validation ensures required fields are completed
- **AND** form submission provides user feedback

#### Scenario: Mailto Button Alternative
- **WHEN** a mailto button is implemented instead of a form
- **THEN** clicking the button opens the user's default email client
- **AND** the email address is pre-filled
- **AND** subject line may be pre-filled

### Requirement: Social Links on Contact Page
The Contact page SHALL display social media/profile links for additional contact methods.

#### Scenario: Social Links Display
- **WHEN** a user views the Contact page
- **THEN** social links are visible and accessible
- **AND** each link opens in a new tab when clicked
- **AND** links are styled consistently with the rest of the site

### Requirement: Responsive Contact Page
The Contact page SHALL be fully responsive and adapt to different screen sizes.

#### Scenario: Mobile Contact Layout
- **WHEN** the Contact page is viewed on mobile
- **THEN** the contact form or mailto button is easily accessible
- **AND** social links are appropriately sized for touch interaction
- **AND** content maintains readable spacing

### Requirement: Professional Contact Page Design
The Contact page SHALL maintain a professional aesthetic consistent with the rest of the portfolio.

#### Scenario: Professional Appearance
- **WHEN** a user views the Contact page
- **THEN** the page presents a clean, professional design
- **AND** contact options are clearly presented
- **AND** the page encourages user engagement

### Requirement: Contact Information Display
The Contact page SHALL support displaying additional contact information such as location or availability when provided.

#### Scenario: Additional Contact Info
- **WHEN** additional contact information is provided
- **THEN** it is displayed in a clear, organized manner
- **AND** information is optional and doesn't clutter the page
